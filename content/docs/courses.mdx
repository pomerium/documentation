# Pomerium Zero - Fundamentals

**Pomerium Zero - Fundamentals** is a series of courses designed to help you understand how Pomerium works so you can secure your apps and services with confidence.

In the span of 10 guides, we'll cover routing, policies, TLS certificates, identity verification, how to manage your Pomerium Zero cluster with the Zero API, and more.

To get started, complete the [**Quickstart guide**](/docs/quickstart) first.

When you're finished, learn how to [**Build Routes**](/docs/courses/zero-fundamentals/build-routes.mdx) in Pomerium Zero.
